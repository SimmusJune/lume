Overview

Lume is a local media library and playback app. This policy explains what data is handled by the app and for what purpose.

Data We Collect

By default, Lume does not collect personal data for advertising or tracking.

Lume may process the following data locally on your device:

  • Imported media library metadata from your JSON files
  • Favorites groups and playlist organization
  • Playback progress and playback statistics
  • Sign-in state for local account or Sign in with Apple session

How Data Is Used

  • Display and manage your media library
  • Enable playback, queue control, and resume behavior
  • Show listening statistics (daily, monthly, yearly)

Tracking and Advertising

Lume does not use your data for cross-app tracking or third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

Lume can load media artwork or media URLs that you import. If those URLs point to third-party hosts, those services may receive standard network request information (such as IP address and user-agent) when content is loaded.

Data Retention

Your library and playback data are retained on your device until you delete the app data or remove content.

Children's Privacy

Lume is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children.
